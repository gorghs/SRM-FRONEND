Recycling Tips

Aluminum cans are highly recyclable! Follow these simple steps to ensure they're processed correctly:

  • Make sure the can is empty.
  • Give it a quick rinse to remove any residue.
  • You can crush the can to save space, but it's not required.
  • Do not place them in plastic bags before putting them in the recycling bin.
